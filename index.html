Google Maps → GPX

Paste a Google Maps directions link (or an already-expanded URL). It decodes the exact stops, routes them along roads, and gives you a .gpx track that the Sideways app will load.

Stops

    Runs entirely in your browser. Short links are expanded through a public CORS proxy (the link is the only thing sent to it); routing uses the public OSRM server. No data is stored. If expansion fails, open the link once and paste the resulting URL here.
